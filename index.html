I received my M.S. of BME from Columbia University this Spring. During my study at Columbia, I focused on medical image analysis based on machine learning and deep learning in Prof. Andrew Laine's group.

I gained my bachelor's from Northeastern University (Shenyang, China) with a major of Biomedical Engineering in 2019. During my undergraduate study, I worked with Dr. Yueyang Teng on construction and analysis of functional brain connectivity based on machine learning and deep learning methods.

Projects

Unravel Developing Infant Brain Network Patterns with Deep Learning
Columbia University; Advisor: Prof. Andrew Laine & Dr. Yun Wang; Oct. 2020 - Feb. 2021
  • Implemented a python script for structure MRI data preprocessing and organized data as BIDS format.
  • Investigated the effect of using different frame displacement calculation parameters at different ages on brain motion detection.
  • Proposing a VAE model for tracking and modelling infant brain pattern changes across the first few years of life in latent space.

Predicting Working Memory Performance Based on Resting State fMRI Data
Neuromatch Academy; Advisor: Dr. Jeff Yau; Jul. 2020
  • Developed a pipeline for fMRI timeseries data processing and functional brain network construction. Implemented Spectral Co-Clustering method to extract four subnetworks from the whole brain network.
  • Proposed a GLM model with L2 regularization to predict participantsâ€™ working memory performances based on extracted subnetworks.

Intrapulmonary Lumen-wall Separation through DE-CT and Deep Learning
Columbia University; Advisor: Dr. Andrew Laine; Apr. 2020 - Sep. 2020
  • Implemented a MATLAB script to extract vessel centerlines and optimize filter results (removed small vessels and disconnected components).
  • Participated in developing an automatic pipeline to separate lumen and wall on DECT scans through multi-material decomposition.
  • Participated in proposing a recursive refinement network (RNN) for 3D registration of expiratory-inspiratory pairs of CT lung scans.

Construction and Analysis of Functional Brain Network Based on Network Similarity
Northeastern University; Advisor: Dr. Yueyang Teng; Mar. 2018 - Apr. 2019
  • Leaded a 3-person student group, made project proposal and coordinated routine work of members.
  • Collected and organized fMRI data from ADNI and ABIDE dataset. Preprocessed data and constructed brain network by DPABI toolbox.
  • Proposed a GCN model for functional brain network classification. Tested the method in ADNI and ABIDE dataset, conducted cross-database experiments and analyzed results.
